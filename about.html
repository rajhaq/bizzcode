What we are all about

Think outside the box, work within the box, turn it into a world – we say. We say it, we mean it, we act on it. Technext is where you turn to for: designing, developing, and supporting your world of websites, web applications, native android & iOS apps. We also work with cross-platform applications in PhoneGap, Unity3D and jQuery mobile. We have a passion for development and prove it by bringing to life our own apps and gaming platforms for everyone in the box to enjoy. We strive to provide a whole new dimension for you to experience. We are always seeking to minimize the distance between client and developer and we ensure only the best quality and highest satisfaction.
